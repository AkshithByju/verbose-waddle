School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution.

The students worked hard to win the inter school debate on environment pollution. It was very difficult, but the amazing students won successfully. They prepared for weeks. Winning first prize is very special, and they definitely deserved it.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition.

Coming second in this competition was great and impressive. These football competitions are very difficult to get even top three, and the school got top two. The principal was very proud of the footballers. The school who got first place were full of football players who were very competitive and very talented.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second prize in inter school Science Project competition.

The students in this school spent time on this project every day to try to win the science competition. The students never gave up, even after encountering countless obstacles. While the school came in second instead of first, it was still an astonishing accomplishment.
